Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Nova Care Transportation is committed to providing a website that is accessible to as many users as possible, including people with disabilities.

Our Goal

We strive to improve usability and accessibility by following recognized best practices and making practical improvements over time.

Accessibility Features We Aim to Support

  • Readable text and clear contrast where practical
  • Responsive layout for mobile and desktop devices
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and interactive controls
  • Meaningful alt text and labels for important UI elements

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We continue reviewing and updating website content and components to reduce barriers.
