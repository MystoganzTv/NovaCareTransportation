Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Nova Care Transportation ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when you use our website and contact form.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a ride request or contact form, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Requested ride date
  • Message details you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your request and coordinate transportation
  • To contact you about scheduling, updates, or follow-up
  • To improve our services and customer support quality
  • To maintain service records and prevent misuse

3. How We Receive Form Submissions

Website form submissions are processed through a third-party form delivery provider (FormSubmit) and forwarded to our business email. By submitting the form, you understand this provider helps transmit your request.

4. What We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Sharing Information

We may share information only when reasonably necessary for business operations, legal compliance, safety, or service fulfillment.

6. Data Retention

We keep submitted information only as long as reasonably needed for communication, scheduling, business records, and legal obligations.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. However, no internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your submitted information by contacting us at enrique.padron853@gmail.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

10. Medical Information Notice

Please do not include unnecessary sensitive medical details in the website form. If needed, we will securely collect only the minimum information required to coordinate transportation.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
